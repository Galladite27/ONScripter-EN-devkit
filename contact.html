Contacting us

The official landing page for the project is on Galladite's site.

We are most commonly active on Discord. If you want help or just to chat, that should be your first call. :-)

The project is hosted on Github. There are issues and PRs there, but we also work informally through the Discord server. But if you don't like Discord and have an issue, please consider reporting it there.

If all else fails, please contact Galladite by e-mail.
